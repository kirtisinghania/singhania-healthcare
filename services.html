Our Services

Comprehensive Women's Healthcare Solutions

🤰 Pregnancy & Maternity Care

🤱

Antenatal Care

Comprehensive prenatal care including regular checkups, monitoring, nutritional guidance, and preparation for childbirth. We support you throughout your pregnancy journey.

⚕️

High Risk Pregnancy

Specialized care for pregnancies requiring extra monitoring due to maternal age, medical conditions, or pregnancy complications. Expert management for optimal outcomes.

💔

Recurrent Pregnancy Loss

Compassionate care and investigation for women experiencing repeated miscarriages. Comprehensive evaluation and treatment planning.

🩺

Pregnancy Related Problems

Management of complications such as gestational diabetes, preeclampsia, placental issues, and other pregnancy-related concerns.

🔬 Diagnostic Services

📡

Ultrasound

Advanced ultrasound imaging for pregnancy monitoring, gynecological assessment, and diagnostic purposes using state-of-the-art equipment.

💓

NST (Non-Stress Test)

Fetal monitoring to assess baby's heart rate and well-being during pregnancy, especially in high-risk cases.

🔬

Pap Smear

Cervical cancer screening test for early detection and prevention of cervical abnormalities.

🧬

Cervical Biopsy

Diagnostic procedure to examine cervical tissue for abnormal cells or conditions.

🩺

Endometrial Biopsy

Tissue sampling from the uterine lining to diagnose abnormal bleeding or other concerns.

🌸 Women's Health Services

👶

Infertility Treatment

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment for couples facing difficulty conceiving. Guidance on fertility enhancement and assisted reproduction options.

💊

Menstrual Disturbances

Treatment for irregular periods, heavy bleeding, painful periods, and other menstrual disorders.

💧

White Discharge

Diagnosis and treatment of abnormal vaginal discharge and related infections.

🎈

Fibroids & Cysts

Management of uterine fibroids and ovarian cysts through medical and surgical approaches.

🚺

Menopause

Comprehensive care for menopausal symptoms including hormonal changes, hot flashes, and related health concerns.

💦

Urinary Complaints

Treatment for urinary incontinence, frequent urination, and other urological concerns in women.

❌

Miscarriage Management

Compassionate care and medical management for pregnancy loss.
